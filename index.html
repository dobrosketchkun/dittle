How to play

Goal

Move all seven of your dice into the opponent’s base column. When someone finishes, both players add up the face-up numbers of their dice in the opponent’s base — highest sum wins.

Setup

Black starts on the left, white on the right. Every die shows 6 on top and 4 toward the opponent.

Advance toward the opposite base

Moves

One move per turn. No backward. No diagonal. No spinning in place.

  • Tilt — tip one step forward or sideways onto an empty square (top face changes).
  • Jump — leap over one or more dice in a straight line onto an empty square (top face stays).
  • Tilt + jump — tip once, then jump (including multi-jumps / L-shapes).

Ghost previews show the top face after your move

Jump details

  • You may jump your own or opponent dice.
  • Landing square must be empty.
  • Jumping several dice needs a gap between them — no leaping a tight cluster.

Scoring

Finishing first is not an automatic win. After the game ends, compare face sums in each opponent’s base. You can keep tilting dice already in the base to raise their numbers.
